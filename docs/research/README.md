# Research

Use `docs/research/` for investigation output, source-driven analysis, experiments, and other research artifacts that are not yet stable architecture contracts.

Organize research under feature-oriented folders such as:

- `docs/research/agent-os/memory/`
- `docs/research/agent-os/session/`
- `docs/research/channels/`

Tasks should stay brief and link to the relevant research output instead of duplicating the full analysis.
